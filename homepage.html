About Us
The Career and Management Club was established in 2012 within the premises of the Middle East Technical University Northern Cyprus Campus. Since its establishment, the Career and Management Club has been among the active communities on campus with the events it has organized and the projects it has carried out.
Vision
To nurture future leaders as a club that stands out with its corporate identity, aims to always be at the forefront with the events and activities it organizes, adapts to current trends, is dynamic, and can touch/inspire the lives of people both within and outside the campus.
Mission
To enable students to lay the foundation stones of their career paths in leadership, management, and entrepreneurship through the events we organize, and to aim for the development of their identities in social spheres, fostering communication with industry professionals and corporate companies.
